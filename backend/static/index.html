Election Information

How It Works

The Phoenix Council election system allows you to vote for 15 council members, with 7 of them serving as executive officers.

Voting Process

  1. Request your voter ID using your registered email and phone number
  2. Check your email for the voter ID
  3. Verify your voter ID in the system
  4. Click on candidate cards to select them for council
  5. Click again on selected candidates to designate as Executive Officers
  6. Click a third time to deselect a candidate
  7. Submit your vote when you've selected 15 candidates (7 as EOs)

Your vote is secure and anonymous. The system uses advanced encryption to protect your privacy.
